A small, peaceful village on the Swiss side of the Portes du Soleil — a short hop from Châtel via Morgins or Champéry. The slopes are uncrowded and the resort runs a dedicated race stadium with permanent gates and timing.

Les Crosets is where we run our **in-season race training weeks** for clubs and committed individuals — gate-running, video review, and tactical coaching with our trainers.
