A working farming village that became a ski resort without losing its character. Châtel sits at the eastern edge of the French Portes du Soleil and is the natural gateway to the Swiss side, Morgins, Champéry, Les Crosets.

Two distinct ski areas: **Linga** (snow-sure, north-facing, links to Avoriaz) and **Super Châtel** (a mellow plateau perfect for beginners and families, and the doorway to Switzerland).
