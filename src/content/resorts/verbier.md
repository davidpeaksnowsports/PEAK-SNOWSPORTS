Sun-drenched south-facing terrace village at the centre of the 4 Vallées, with Mont Fort (3300 m) topping out an area built for skiers who like big lines, steep faces, and a strong après scene.

Verbier hosts the Xtreme Verbier, the closing event of the Freeride World Tour, and the off-piste reputation is hard-earned. Our **Verbier ski camp** launches in 2027 for confident skiers chasing freeride terrain with structured coaching alongside.
