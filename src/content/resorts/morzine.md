A traditional Alpine village at the heart of the Portes du Soleil, one of the largest connected ski areas in the world. Easy lift access from the centre of resort, a network of beginner-friendly slopes around the Pleney, and quick links to Avoriaz, Les Gets, and over the Swiss side via Châtel.

Morzine is where we run the bulk of our private lessons, group lessons, and kids' camps. If you're booking your first Peak lesson, this is most likely where you'll meet us.
