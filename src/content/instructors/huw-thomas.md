I got into skiing a little later than most but its become my favourite sport and I have enjoyed teaching it so much over the past 10 years. I feel very lucky to be able to pass on my enthusiasm for it to all ages and abilities, and be able to do that in some really beautiful places. Sharing these experiences is a big part as to why I enjoy it so much.

I studied sport science at Bangor University and since then I’ve made my home for the winter in the alps teaching skiing and in Pembrokeshire in the summer trying to spend as much time outside with friends as I can. I am a calm and patient person and I will adapt my teaching to suit each client hopefully resulting in good times for all!

Take your time finding comfortable ski boots. If you’re in discomfort it makes it very difficult to have a good time.
