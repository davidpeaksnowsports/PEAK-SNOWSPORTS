## Aries — Mar 21 to Apr 19

Bold and full of energy, you're charging straight for that black run. Confidence is great. So are knees. Warm up first.

## Taurus — Apr 20 to May 20

You're all about comfort, which means extra hot chocolate breaks this week. That's fine — après is a sport too.

## Gemini — May 21 to Jun 20

Two personalities, two ski styles. One wants to bomb it, the other to stop and take photos. This week, you can do both — just not at the same time.

## Cancer — Jun 21 to Jul 22

Your nurturing side comes out on the slopes. Expect to be the group's glove-finder, snack-provider, and morale-booster.

## Leo — Jul 23 to Aug 22

Spotlight's on you — literally, you're the one in the neon jacket. Own it, but maybe keep the dramatic flourishes to the après dance floor.

## Virgo — Aug 23 to Sep 22

Everything is organised: lift pass in sleeve pocket, snacks perfectly portioned, goggles spotless. This week, loosen up. A little snow in the plan won't hurt.

## Libra — Sep 23 to Oct 22

You're torn between pistes and powder. This week the mountain says: do both, but bring friends to help carry your snacks.

## Scorpio — Oct 23 to Nov 21

You've got the intense focus of a racer. Just don't forget to enjoy the view. The Alps aren't only a training ground.

## Sagittarius — Nov 22 to Dec 21

Adventure calls. This week is perfect for exploring that hidden run your instructor mentioned. Just make sure it's open. And legal.

## Capricorn — Dec 22 to Jan 19

Determined and disciplined, you're nailing every turn. But remember: success also includes knowing when to stop for fondue.

## Aquarius — Jan 20 to Feb 18

Ever the innovator, you're trying something new — maybe ski touring, maybe snowboard. This week, your creativity will keep things fresh.

## Pisces — Feb 19 to Mar 20

Dreamy and intuitive, you'll find the perfect line through the snow this week. Just don't drift off so much you miss the last lift.
